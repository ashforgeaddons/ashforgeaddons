Privacy

AshForge add-ons are designed to be lightweight and local-first.

General privacy approach

  • AshForge add-ons are built to store settings locally in Firefox whenever possible.
  • The add-ons are not designed to sell or share personal data.
  • Some features need Firefox permissions such as bookmarks, history, tabs, storage, or management to perform their stated function.
  • When an add-on uses outside services, those services may receive normal web requests from your browser.

AshForge Addons enhances Firefox with focused tools that build on existing browser features instead of replacing them.
