Quick Home

Adds a second customizable home-page button to Firefox.

Overview

Quick Home gives Firefox a second home button concept without replacing the normal Home button.

Features

  • Set a second home page URL.
  • Open in the current tab or a new tab.
  • Use a custom toolbar icon.
  • Links into the AshForge ecosystem.
