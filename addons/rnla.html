RNLA - Read Now, Later, Always

A saved-content manager for read-now, read-later, and always-keep links.

Overview

RNLA helps organize saved links beyond normal bookmarks. It is the larger, more advanced saved-content tool in the AshForge family.

Features

  • Save articles, videos, websites, and other links.
  • Organize saved content outside normal bookmarks.
  • Use import/export for portability.
  • Works with other AshForge tools such as Theater Time and the Hub.
