Scroll Forge

Fine-tune scrolling behavior and scrollbar themes.

Overview

Scroll Forge focuses on making scrolling feel better and more customizable.

Features

  • Tune scrolling behavior.
  • Customize webpage scrollbar visuals.
  • Keep the focus on scroll-related improvements.
  • Future direction: scroll profiles and per-site tuning.
