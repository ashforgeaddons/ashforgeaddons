Theater Time

A visual video bookmark library for saved video links.

Overview

Theater Time turns saved video links into a visual library with thumbnails and quick launch options.

Features

  • Scan Firefox bookmarks and history for video links.
  • Import from supported AshForge tools.
  • Use grid, list, and preview-card views.
  • Open videos reliably on their source site when embeds are blocked.
